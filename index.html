Hi! I'm Stacey Culligan

Let’s talk creatives. I’ve had an interesting career path, from archaeologist and illustrator, to ETF data analyst and now UX designer. The most important way I can describe myself is technically creative. I love noodling through how to code a macro, finding stories in data and finding creative ways to solve problems or discover niches for products. I will not be the visual designer unicorn that is a multi-media artist in her spare time, but I will make magic within technical or data constraints.

My Work

The Lost Expedition

The Lost Expedition is a solo UX case study. It’s aimed at motivating users to exercise more, by gamifying their experience into a mystery narrative and visual/camera-based scavenger hunt. This project took me all the way from initial user interviews to user testing on a high-fidelity clickable prototype.

Lexi

Lexi is a vocabulary learning app UX case study. The app is low fidelity but includes a clickable prototype and is the result of user testing and interviews. Lexi is aimed at homeschoolers, both parents and students to help improve their vocabulary learning process.
